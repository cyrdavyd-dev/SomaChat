Privacy Policy

Soma Chat · Package ID com.kirilldavydov.somachat

Last updated: April 12, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile application Soma Chat (“the App”) processes information on your device. The App is designed for local health-related journaling in a chat-style interface (messages, vitals, symptoms, prescriptions, and related attachments).

2. Data we process

Depending on what you enter, the App may store on your device:

  • Profile data: display name, date of birth, height, weight, gender (as selected), blood type, country/region key, allergies, conditions, medications, and a local path to a profile photo if you choose one.
  • Chat content: message text, timestamps, message type, and attached files (copies kept in the App’s private data area).
  • App preferences: e.g. the identifier of the active chat thread, stored locally to restore your session.

Processing happens on your device. The current version of the App does not provide user accounts or cloud sync to the developer’s servers.

3. Where data is stored

Data is stored locally using a SQLite database and files under the application’s app data directory on your device. Temporary files may be created in the cache (for example when generating exports).

The App does not transmit your health or chat data to the operator’s servers for storage. There is no in-app analytics or crash-reporting SDK sending personal data to the developer in the current release.

4. Sharing and third parties

The operator does not receive your health data from the App by default. Data may leave your device when you initiate an action, for example:

  • Export / Share: PDF or related files via the operating system share sheet — you choose the receiving app (email, messenger, cloud storage, etc.).
  • Open attachment: opening a file in another app using OS features.
  • Device backup: depending on your device settings, application data may be included in system backups (including cloud backup where enabled).

5. Network and permissions

On Android, the App manifest may declare network-related permissions commonly used by the platform. The App does not use these permissions to upload your medical records to the operator’s servers.

Access to photos for a profile picture and files for attachments is requested only through standard system pickers when you choose to add content.

6. Security and your responsibility

Protect your device (screen lock, trusted apps). The operator cannot guarantee the confidentiality of data if the device is compromised or if you share exported files with others.

7. Children

The App is not directed at collecting data from children. If a parent or guardian enters information about a child, they do so voluntarily and under their own responsibility.

8. Changes

This Policy may be updated if the App’s functionality changes. The current version will be published at the same URL you use for the store listing.
